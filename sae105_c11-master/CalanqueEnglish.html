”Diving in the heart of the Corsican Calanques”

Discover the Corsican creeks by diving into a biodiversity
overflowing with a unique and protected heritage

Ratings :

Rate :

Between 750 and 950€

Summary of the stay :

“Discover the creeks of Piana, with their granite rocks, their extraordinary colors and clear waters. go away dive at the foot of the cliffs and observe a great diversity of Mediterranean fish: sarres, wrasse, groupers, moray eels, ... In this protected reserve, you will appreciate the sweetness Mediterranean and its unique heritage.”

  • Clear and warm waters

age range :

Service not included in the price :

  • Transport to Ajaccio
  • Drinks at the bar

Detailed program :

  • Arrival day: reception at Ajaccio airport at 10 a.m. and bus transport to the center. Hike to the “head of dog” to appreciate the spectacular views of the creeks

  • Days 2 to 4: 2 daily dives on the most beautiful sites diving in the creeks with the club's boat

  • Day 5: hike in the Ayton forest with swimming in the basins of torrents in the morning, mountain biking on the forest roads

  • Day 6: swimming on Porto beach and tasting meal local products (charcuterie, cheese, chestnut cream). Departure from the airport at 6 p.m.
