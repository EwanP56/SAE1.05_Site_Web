Description of accommodation:

The sports village is located in the Gulf of Ajaccio, 300 m from the Argent beach renowned for its clear waters. Bordered by the maquis, you will enjoy a natural environment where the Mediterranean vegetation unfolds as far as the eye can see.

Accommodation facilities:

  • Nautical base 10 minutes walk from your accommodation
  • Outdoor pool
  • Fitness room

Type of accommodation :

  • bungalows for 4 people: possibility of 2 people with supplement

Catering:

  • self-service or packed lunch on request
